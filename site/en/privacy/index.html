Privacy policy

MouBCN is designed to work without accounts, advertising, analytics, or tracking.

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Summary: preferences and saved places remain on your device. Location is only used with permission while the app is open and is not stored on MouBCN servers.

Controller

MouBCN is a personal project developed by Oriol Carbó. For privacy questions, contact oriol.carbo+moubcn@gmail.com.

Data stored on your device

The app may locally store language, appearance, alert filters, Home and Work, favourite lines and stops, recent activity, and the last map selection. This data is not uploaded to an account or to MouBCN servers.

You can remove Home or Work individually, use “Delete personal app data” in Settings, or uninstall the app.

Location

If you allow it, MouBCN accesses location only while you use the app to centre the map, show nearby stops, and use your position as the origin or destination of a route.

When you use these features, the required coordinates are temporarily sent over HTTPS to the MouBCN backend. The planner may send the origin and destination to TMB, and the operating system may process a coordinate to create an address label. MouBCN does not retain these coordinates on its server and removes them from logs.

Technical data and security

Network connections temporarily transmit an IP address, as any internet service does. MouBCN does not store it in logs. The abuse limiter keeps it in memory only for a one-minute request window and then removes it.

Providers

MouBCN uses information from TMB, FGC, and TRAM Barcelona, along with map and geocoding services supplied by the device platform. See the data sources and their conditions.

Purpose and legal basis

Location is processed with your permission and only to perform the feature you request. Local data is processed to provide personalisation and shortcuts within the app.

Retention, control, and rights

  • Local data remains until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Backend coordinates only exist while the request is being served.
  • You can revoke location access in iOS or Android Settings.
  • You can ask for information or exercise applicable rights through the contact email.

Changes

If MouBCN adds accounts, analytics, advertising, or a new form of processing, this policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated before the change is released.
